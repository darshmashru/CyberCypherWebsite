What is Cyber Cypher ?

Cyber Cypher is an 18-hour hackathon that aims to provide a platform to both budding and experienced coders alike. We intend to target engineering students from every skill-group, ranging from students with surface-level to the ones with ample coding experience. Cyber Cypher intends to make the participants appreciate the limitless potential that lies dormant within them with respect to this dynamic field of computer sciences.
