About our University

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal or SVKM is a Public Charitable Trust founded in 1934. Over the past 80 years, it has had more than 35,000 students and ever since its humble beginnings in Mumbai, it has now spread its wings to other major metro cities.SVKM's NMIMS with a legacy of 36 years has emerged as a multi-disciplinary University with 7 campuses spread across the country, that includes Management, Engineering, and various other fields. Its prestigious constituent school - Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management & Engineering has introduced first of its kind courses like Data Science Artificial Intelligence along with Mechatronics, Cybersecurity, Electronics and Telecommunications and other courses in Academic Collaboration with Virginia Tech… and has also been awarded several accolades for the same.

NMIMS' Official Hackathon

What is Cyber Cypher

Cyber Cypher is an 18-hour hackathon that aims to provide a platform to both budding and experienced coders alike.We intend to target engineering students from every skill-group, ranging from students with surface-level to the ones with ample coding experience. Cyber Cypher intends to make the participants appreciate the limitless potential that lies dormant within them with respect to this dynamic field of computer sciences.
